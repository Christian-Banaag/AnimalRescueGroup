Animal Rescue Group

How We Started

Our organization was founded to provide temporary shelter, food, medical attention, and comfort to displaced and neglected companion animals and educate the public about the humane care of animals with a goal of reducing overpopulation.

With your support we have found loving homes for many animals and come to the aide of countless others that rely on our help.

Our facility houses displaced, unwanted, and stray animals all looking for new homes.

We hope you will come visit us soon!

Our Mission

The mission of the Animal Rescue Group is to provide temporary shelter, food, medical attention, and comfort to displaced and neglected companion animals and educate the public about the humane care of animals with a goal of reducing overpopulation.

There are many ways you can be a part of our mission; Volunteering, donating, foster care, and animal socializing are just a few of the many ways you can show your support for the Animal Rescue Group.

Where You Come In

There are many ways you can help. Socializing cats, walking dogs, fostering animals, in-kind and cash donations are just a few of the ways you can help us fulfill our mission.
